Our Vision

Citizen-Centered Design applies approaches from Human-Centered Design to the realm of government. Our goal is to create a toolkit that can equip you as government employees with Citizen-Centered Design methods that you can use within your community to improve the delivery of government services.

We are excited to help you champion innovation within government. Our toolkit is an ongoing effort, but we have started with sharing how you can use Citizen-Centered Design improve an existing service. With each step, you will also be able to download a worksheet that you can use start using Citizen-Centered Design with your team. Scroll below to get started.
